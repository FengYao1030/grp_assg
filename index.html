    Crime in Malaysia

    Updated 2025

    Types of Crime

    Crimes in Malaysia can be categorized into several major types such as violent crimes, property crimes, cybercrimes, and drug-related offenses. Each category has unique characteristics and impact on society.

    • Violent crimes: robbery, assault, homicide
    • Property crimes: burglary, theft, fraud
    • Cybercrimes: hacking, phishing, scams

    Statistics of Crime

    Based on the Royal Malaysia Police (PDRM) and national crime reports, crime rates have shown fluctuations over the years, with cybercrime cases increasing rapidly in recent times.
